Chicken Curry

Description

This is the Malaysian version of Indian chicken curry, which uses coconut milk. It can be substituted with yogurt or low-fat milk, but the taste won't be the same.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Cardamom pod
  • Star anise pod
  • Curry leaves
  • Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Curry paste
  • Salt
